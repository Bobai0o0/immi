Questions?

Is it only for learning English?

Currently, we focus on English as the target language, but we offer native language support/UI in Spanish, Arabic, French, Ukrainian, and Chinese.

Does it replace human help?

No. immi is a tool to empower you. For complex legal or medical advice, we always recommend professional human services, which we can help connect you to.

How does the camera feature work?

Simply point your phone at any text. Our AI recognizes the text, translates it, and provides a "Plain Language" summary of what it means.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not store your audio recordings or camera scans. All processing is done with strict privacy protocols.

Is it free?

We have a robust free tier for essential daily tasks. A premium subscription unlocks unlimited roleplay and advanced document scanning.
