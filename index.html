The Reality

Bridging the Gap

Language barriers are life barriers, which only get worse.

A lack of proficiency in the destination country's langauge creates gaps of vulnerability and exclusion, leaving them unable to appreciate the destination country.

  • Restricted Access: Difficulty securing stable housing, healthcare, and fair employment.
  • Social Isolation: The loneliness of living in a world where you can't express your true self.
  • Cultural Friction: The constant fear of losing your native identity while trying to adapt.

Generic tools lack application and personalization.

Traditional apps treat language just as text, but immi recognizes the need for comfort and the asset of your heritage.

  • No Context: They teach "The apple is red" when you need to know "Why does this lease ask for a co-signer?"
  • Decontextualized: Lessons feel like homework, not real-world preparation for a doctor visit.
  • Culturally Blind: Ignoring the lived experience, age, and unique accents of the user.

Questions?

Is it only for learning English?

Currently, we focus on English as the target language, but we offer native language support/UI in Spanish, Arabic, French, Ukrainian, and Chinese. Future updated will allow users to pinpoint exact langauges and dialects as we offer a greater data range.

Does it replace human help?

No. immi is a tool to empower you. For complex legal or medical advice, we always recommend professional human services, which we can help connect you to.

How does the camera feature work?

Simply point your phone at any text. Our AI recognizes the text, translates it, and provides a "Plain Language" summary of what it means.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not store your audio recordings or camera scans. All processing is done with strict privacy protocols.

Is it free?

We have a robust free tier for essential daily tasks. A premium subscription unlocks unlimited roleplay and advanced document scanning.
